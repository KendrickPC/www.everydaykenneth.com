Chao's Birthday Dinner

Today I woke up late because it was Chao's birthday so we celebrated at a Japanese restaurant in Ximen. Dinner started at 20:00, but James and I arrived late due to his business meeting in Taipei. Once we arrived, we ordered small plate varieties of different Japanese food and started drinking Sake. By the end of the night, everyone went home really drunk.

When I woke up this morning, I realized how that drinking in Asia is definitely a challenge for me seeing as how drinking is such a huge part of Asian business culture.

I think what's most significant about this week is how much work I have to finish up. I realize that a lot of people are depending on me for the completion of my projects. Therefore, I have to complete a couple of projects by the end of this month, not to mention keeping up with my regular work load.

This month, I am making a commitment to my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. Life is definitely very lonely here in Taipei, Taiwan. I have no family here and my closest friends are still back home in the Bay Area. However, I understand that God wants me here right now and I'm here for a reason.
