2017 Summer Update

2017 has been an interesting year for me so far. I’ve met a whole new group of friends, had a productive year coding cool projects that I’m really passionate about, and attended a couple of wedding parties. Surprisingly, the time has gone by very quickly. I’ve gone camping, learned new piano songs, travelled to Hong Kong, and started codeTaiwan.org with a couple of business partners. Overall, I’d say it’s been a very productive year and I’m really proud of my web development projects.
